.container

This is a fixed-width container. It changes size at different breakpoints.

.container-fluid

This container always spans the full width of the viewport.

.container-sm (fluid until small ≥576px)
.container-md (fluid until medium ≥768px)
.container-lg (fluid until large ≥992px)
.container-xl (fluid until extra large ≥1200px)
.container-xxl (fluid until extra extra large ≥1400px)
